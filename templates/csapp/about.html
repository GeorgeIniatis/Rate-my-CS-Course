About us

We are a small group of Computer Science students that were tired of choosing our courses based on their description and nothing more. We wanted to be able to hear what students have to say about each course, in order to provide us and every other Computer Science student a more useful guide on choosing our courses for the following year. We came up with the idea to give students a platform where they could freely express how they feel about the course and rate each course in order to help other students. Students are able to rate their current courses based on:

  • their overall satisfaction with the course
  • their satisfaction with the lecturer
  • how engaging the course is
  • how informative the course is
  • and also leave comments about the course
